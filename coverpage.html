FROSH CARNIVAL

Frosh carnival, a twirly ride of endless memories and electrifying experiences instilled with highs and lows that rekindles amongst us the childhood memories. However, this ride of life never fails to overwhelm us with its unexpected and unfortunate turn of events. Yet, it is important to remember that, “After every dusk, there is a new dawn for us to shine brighter than ever before, like never before.

Though the carnival lights will always shine, once missed chances, can’t turn back time. A place where you let your highs and lows guide you to the light of wisdom as you dance among the heights and dig deeper in the ocean, awaits for you. Frosh brings to you a roller coaster of exhilarating experiences and timeless memories, a ride to capture your hearts and resurrect your souls in the form of a powerful enigma of fun, frolic and laughter as we present to you the Frosh Carnival.
